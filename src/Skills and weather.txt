WEATHER SYSTEM

🌡️ TEMPERATURE
Mission	                Base	   Range
Training/Fire Season	22°C	-10 to +50°C
Pine Ridge	            24°C	-10 to +50°C
Wildfire Front	        28°C	-10 to +50°C

Temperature	   Burn Duration	Fire Radius	   Spread Modifier
10°C	       18.0s	       10 px	       ×0.80 (minimum)
22°C	       15.0s	       22 px	       ×0.94 (typical)
28°C	       13.5s	       28 px	       ×1.00 (hotter)
50°C	        8.0s	       50 px	       ×1.25 (extreme!)


Effects:
Burn Duration: burnDuration = max(6, 18 − (temp − 10) × 0.25) — higher temp = faster burnout, shorter fire duration
Fire Radius: baseRadius = max(3, temp) — radius in px equals temperature in °C (10°C=10px, 50°C=50px)
Spread Probability: tempMod = max(0.5, 0.8 + (temp − 10) × 0.01125) — linear scale, 10°C = ×0.80, 28°C = ×1.00, 50°C = ×1.25


💨 HUMIDITY   (smooth linear scale)
Humidity	 Condition	    Spread Modifier
80%	         Very Damp	    ×0.05 (minimum)
55%	         Moderate	    ×0.66
40%	         Dry	        ×1.02
25%	         Very Dry	    ×1.39
10%	         Extreme Dry	×1.75 (maximum)

Formula: humidMod = max(0.05, min(1.75, 0.05 + (80 − humidity) × (1.70 / 70)))

🌪️ WIND   (scale: 1–100 integer)
Wind affects ONLY spread radius — NOT spread probability.

Wind Conditions & Radius Added (at maximum downwind)
1–9:    Barely noticeable   (+1–5 px downwind)
10–30:  Light breeze        (+5–15 px downwind)
31–50:  Moderate wind       (+16–25 px downwind)
51–70:  Strong wind         (+26–35 px downwind)
71–90:  Very strong wind    (+36–45 px downwind)
91–100: Extreme             (+46–50 px downwind)

Base Values:
Training/Fire Season: 30
Pine Ridge: 30
Wildfire Front: 10 (more survivable)

Escalation Model:
Wind increases +10 per 10 minutes of mission time

Formula: currentWind = baseWind + floor(elapsedTime / 600) × 10
Example: After 20 minutes, +20 to base wind

Directional Effects (Wind-Adjusted Spread Radius):
windAddedRadius = max(1, (windStrength / 100) × 50)   → 1px at wind=1, 50px at wind=100
Downwind: spreadRadius = baseRadius + windAddedRadius
Upwind:   spreadRadius = baseRadius (no bonus or penalty upwind)
Formula: windAdjustedRadius = baseRadius + windAddedRadius × max(0, cos(windDifference))
Where windInfluence = cos(angleDifference) ranges -1 (dead upwind) to +1 (dead downwind)

Wind in Missions:
Angle and strength both vary (debug keys: Q/E for angle, K/L for strength ±5)
Optional randomizeWind: true flag varies wind throughout mission
FIRE SPREAD PROBABILITY (MASTER FORMULA)
Formula: spreadChance = baseSpreadChance × tempMod × humidMod
(Wind affects radius only — downwind fire reaches further, not more likely per attempt)

Example:
28°C (hot), 25% humidity (dry) → tempMod ×1.00 × humidMod ×1.39 = ~×1.39 baseline spread chance
20°C (cool), 60% humidity (damp) → tempMod ×0.91 × humidMod ×0.54 = ~×0.49 baseline spread chance
